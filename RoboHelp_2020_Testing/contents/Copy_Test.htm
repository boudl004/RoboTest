Talking Points:

 

Refer to the talking points below if receiving calls regarding the incident at AHC Buffalo Crossroads:

Note: All news media inquiries should be referred to Conny Bergeson, Public Relations director, at 612-270-9935.

  • If asked questions about the incident: "Thank you for your question. I am not in a position to comment on the details of this incident."
  • If offering sympathy or other support: "Thank you so much for your words of support of our extended Allina Health family at this very difficult time."
  • If questions about upcoming appointments at Allina Health Clinic - Buffalo Crossroads: "The Buffalo Crossroads clinic is closed until further notice. Can I help you reschedule your appointment at a different location?"
  • Questions about where providers will be moved: "We are in the process of temporarily relocating providers to other locations but do not have that information yet. Check our website at allinahealth.org or social media for any latest updates on closures."
  • General questions about other locations: Refer to list of closures and help address specific needs.
  • Questions about if our facilities are safe to receive services at: "Yes, our facilities are safe to come in and receive care. We take every reasonable precaution to safeguard our patients and staff and work closely with our Corporate Security team."
  • Will my provider be checking messages, such as medical messages and prescription refills? "We have arranged for care teams at other Allina Health clinic sites to follow-up on messages, refills, etc."
  • What do I need to do if my orthopedic injection that requires the administration be provided by the same group/providers? "Please contact your health insurance provider on the phone number located on the back of your health insurance card for guidance."
